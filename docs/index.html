Welcome to Remak

What's Remak?

Remak is a professional résumé creation application designed for the Windows® operating system.

How does Remak work?

1. Enter your email address.

2. You will then be prompted to enter a verification code, sent to the provided address, to confirm ownership. Below is an example of the email you will receive:

reMak — Secure Access Verification

Hello,

We received a request to verify your identity for your reMak account.

XXXXXX

Please enter this code in the reMak app within the next 10 minutes to complete your sign-in.

If you did not request this code, you can safely ignore this message and no changes will be made to your account.

Stay secure,
The reMak Team

3. Upon entering the verification code, you may proceed to either:

Complete the form with your personal and professional details, or

Select a résumé template and generate your résumé instantly. Once your résumé is finalized, it can be exported as a PDF.

Email Delivery Policy

If, for any reason (such as a full mailbox or an invalid email address), the verification email cannot be delivered on the first attempt, it will not be resent automatically.

Any new verification email must be triggered by a fresh login attempt. We do not store, maintain, or use any mailing lists for any purpose whatsoever.
